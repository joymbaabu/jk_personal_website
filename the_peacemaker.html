The Peace Maker

Peace holds a different meaning to everyone. It is a personal and subjective  experience. TO some, it is merely absence of war and violence. To many others, it is the presence of justice, fairness, harmony and serenity. To yet others, it is constructive resolution of conflict as and when they come.

I have been called and trained to help in building peace, especially through conflict resolution. I work with people experiencing internal or inner conflict, and help them find inner peace. I work with people experiencing inter personal conflict, and help them, through mediation, to find a win-win solution that meets their current and future needs and interests, and possibly helps them forge a better relationship.

I also work with communities in conflict searching for peaceful coexistence. We change paradigms from scarcity to abundance, from they verses we to us. The world is a much better place to live and thrive when people experience intra, inter and community peace.
