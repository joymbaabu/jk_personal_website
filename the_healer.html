The Healer

At the core of Jesus’ ministry was healing. Jesus healed broken and sick bodies, hearts and spirits. He healed people who had faced adversity, rejection, abuse, humiliation, domination, manipulation, neglect and all sorts of pain and brokenness. He healed through compassionate listening, caring, speaking, touching and presence. Jesus restored dignity, hope, courage, confidence, resilience, love, hope faith and blessed assurance for a better future-now and into eternity.

Jesus has called me to join Him in the ministry of healing. I have, with deep humility and thankfulness, heeded to His call, and have been commissioned (ordained by the Church of Christ at Karen Community Church) to do that. I am a healing-healer. I am committed to do good, everywhere l go, with God’s help.
