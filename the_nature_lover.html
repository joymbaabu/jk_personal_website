Nature Lover

Nature is healing and nourishing.

I frequently spend time in natural settings to rewind and refresh my mind and soul. It is also invigorating to the body and spirit. Walking mindfully through nature trails brings out the wonder and awesomeness of God and the beauty of his creation.

The birds, trees, insects. rocks. plants etc. all point to this wonder, and bring to mind the caring nature of God and the need for simplicity and contentment.

My favorite local nature attraction is www.pecnaturecamp.com
